About Me

Get to know more about me and my journey in design and development.

Creative Designer & Developer

I'm Imesh Rathnayaka, a passionate graphic designer and web developer based in Sri Lanka. With a keen eye for aesthetics and a love for clean code, I bridge the gap between design and technology.

My journey began with a fascination for visual communication, which evolved into a career creating digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. I believe every pixel matters and every line of code should serve a purpose.

When I'm not designing or coding, you'll find me exploring new design trends, contributing to open-source projects, or enjoying a good cup of coffee while sketching new ideas.
